Startup Grants for African Founders

Access non-dilutive funding designed to help early and growth-stage startups scale. Grants are primarily awarded in Naira, with local currency equivalents available for non-Nigerian founders.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Residency: Must be based in Africa or part of the African diaspora
  • Age: 18 – 45 years (Youth Grant capped at 35)
  • Stage: Early‑stage or growth‑stage startups with clear traction or MVP
  • Sector: Open to all industries, with priority for tech, health, climate, and social impact

These criteria ensure grants reach founders who are ready to build, scale, and create measurable impact. Female‑led and impact‑driven startups are strongly encouraged to apply.

Application Process

Our structured evaluation process identifies founders with high potential, strong execution ability, and real problem understanding.

1

Application

Submit your startup idea, problem, solution, traction (if any), and founder profile. Clarity matters more than complexity.

⏱ 10–15 mins • Required

2

Initial Screening

Our system and review team filter applications based on clarity, problem strength, and execution potential.

📊 Shortlisting stage

3

Deep Evaluation

Shortlisted founders are evaluated on business model strength, market size, and ability to execute.

⚖️ Scored review process

4

Selection & Funding

Selected founders receive funding, mentorship, and entry into the FSI ecosystem.

🏆 Final decision stage

Selection is competitive. We prioritize founders who demonstrate clarity, urgency, and strong understanding of the problem they are solving.
