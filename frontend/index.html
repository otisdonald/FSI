About FSI

The Founders Support Initiative (FSI) exists to turn ambitious ideas into impactful, scalable businesses across Africa. Too many promising ventures never leave the drawing board due to lack of capital, guidance, or the right network. FSI was created to close that gap.

What We Do

  • Funding: Connect founders to early-stage capital.
  • Mentorship: Pair with experienced African operators.
  • Training: Cohort-based programs covering validation, go-to-market, and fundraising.

Our Mission

To unlock the next generation of African innovators by bridging the gap between ideas and execution. We back ventures that are profitable, scalable, and sustainable — businesses that grow across borders and create meaningful jobs.

Why FSI Exists

  • Validation Gap: Helping founders test and refine ideas.
  • Execution Gap: Providing playbooks, tools, and support.
  • Network Gap: Opening doors to partners, customers, and investors.

Who We Support

Ambitious founders across Africa at the ideation to early stage. Whether you’re a first-time founder, a student with a bold concept, or a professional ready to go full-time — if you’re committed to building something that matters, FSI is for you.

The FSI Promise

We don’t just fund. We build alongside you.
We don’t just teach. We connect you to people who’ve done it before.
We don’t just talk impact. We back ventures that solve real problems for real people.

Your idea is valid. Let’s make it real.

Selection Process

We don’t accept everyone. Our structured evaluation process identifies founders with high potential, strong execution ability, and real problem understanding.

1

Application

You submit your startup idea, problem, solution, traction (if any), and founder profile. Clarity matters more than complexity.

⏱ 10–15 mins • Required

2

Initial Screening

Our system and review team filter applications based on clarity, problem strength, and execution potential.

📊 Shortlisting stage

3

Deep Evaluation

Shortlisted founders are evaluated on business model strength, market size, and ability to execute.

⚖️ Scored review process

4

Selection & Funding

Selected founders receive funding, mentorship, and entry into the FSI ecosystem.

🏆 Final decision stage

Selection is competitive. We prioritize founders who demonstrate clarity, urgency, and strong understanding of the problem they are solving.
