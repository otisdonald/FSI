About the Founders Support Initiative

Empowering Africa’s Next Generation of Builders

The Founders Support Initiative (FSI) exists to turn ambitious ideas into impactful, scalable businesses across Africa. We believe Africa’s greatest asset is its people and their ideas. But too many promising ventures never leave the drawing board due to lack of capital, guidance, or the right network. FSI was created to close that gap.

What We Do

We equip early-stage founders with the three things they need most: funding, mentorship, and training.

  • Access to Funding: We connect founders to early-stage capital and investors so they can build their MVP, run pilots, and validate their market without depleting personal savings.
  • Hands-on Mentorship: Our founders are paired with experienced African operators and entrepreneurs who’ve built and scaled companies. You get real-world advice, accountability, and direct introductions that matter.
  • Structured Training Programs: From idea validation to go-to-market strategy and fundraising, our cohort-based programs give founders practical frameworks to avoid common pitfalls and move faster with confidence.

Our Mission

To unlock the next generation of African innovators by bridging the gap between ideas and execution. Africa doesn’t lack ideas. It lacks infrastructure — the support systems that make execution possible. FSI provides that infrastructure. We back founders who are solving real African problems in fintech, agri-tech, health-tech, climate, logistics, education, and beyond.

Our focus is on ventures that are not just profitable, but scalable and sustainable — businesses that can grow across borders and create meaningful jobs.

Why FSI Exists

  • The Validation Gap: Most startups fail because they build products no one needs. We help founders test and refine their ideas before they spend years and capital on the wrong problem.
  • The Execution Gap: Great ideas need great execution. We provide the playbooks, tools, and support to get there.
  • The Network Gap: Who you know is often as important as what you know. FSI opens doors to partners, customers, and investors.

Who We Support

Ambitious founders across Africa at the ideation to early stage. Whether you’re a first-time founder, a student with a bold concept, or a professional ready to go full-time — if you’re committed to building something that matters, FSI is for you.

Selection Process

We don’t accept everyone. We follow a structured evaluation process to identify founders with high potential, strong execution ability, and real problem understanding.

1. Application

You submit your startup idea, problem, solution, traction (if any), and founder profile. Clarity matters more than complexity.

⏱ 10–15 mins • Required

2. Initial Screening

Our system and review team filter applications based on clarity, problem strength, and execution potential.

📊 Shortlisting stage

3. Deep Evaluation

Shortlisted founders are evaluated on business model strength, market size, and ability to execute.

Scored review process

4. Selection & Funding

Selected founders receive funding, mentorship, and entry into the FSI ecosystem.

Final decision stage

Selection is competitive. We prioritize founders who demonstrate clarity, urgency, and strong understanding of the problem they are solving.
