About Founders Support Initiative

The Founders Support Initiative (FSI) is dedicated to empowering ambitious entrepreneurs across Africa with the resources they need to build impactful and scalable businesses.

We provide access to funding, mentorship, and structured training programs designed to help founders navigate early-stage challenges, validate their ideas, and grow sustainable ventures.

Our mission is to unlock the next generation of African innovators by creating opportunities that bridge the gap between ideas and execution.

Selection Process

We don’t accept everyone. We follow a structured evaluation process to identify founders with high potential, strong execution ability, and real problem understanding.

1. Application

You submit your startup idea, problem, solution, traction (if any), and founder profile. Clarity matters more than complexity.

⏱ 10–15 mins • Required

2. Initial Screening

Our system and review team filter applications based on clarity, problem strength, and execution potential.

📊 Shortlisting stage

3. Deep Evaluation

Shortlisted founders are evaluated on business model strength, market size, and ability to execute.

🧠 Scored review process

4. Selection & Funding

Selected founders receive funding, mentorship, and entry into the FSI ecosystem.

🎯 Final decision stage

Selection is competitive. We prioritize founders who demonstrate clarity, urgency, and strong understanding of the problem they are solving.
