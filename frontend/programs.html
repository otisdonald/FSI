Founder Support Initiative

Our Programs

We provide comprehensive support for African founders: funding, mentorship, and training designed to help you start strong, scale sustainably, and lead with confidence.

Funding

Access non-dilutive capital in Naira to build and scale your startup. Our funding programs are designed to give founders the financial runway they need without losing equity.

  • Seed grants for early-stage startups
  • Flexible use of funds for product, marketing, or operations
  • Open to all industries and sectors
  • Competitive selection based on clarity and execution potential

Mentorship

Learn directly from experienced founders, industry leaders, and global operators. Our mentorship connects you with people who have built, scaled, and exited successful ventures.

  • 1-on-1 mentorship sessions tailored to your startup stage
  • Access to global experts across industries
  • Peer learning groups for shared growth
  • Guidance on fundraising, product-market fit, and scaling

Training

Structured startup building and leadership development programs. We equip founders with the skills, tools, and mindset needed to thrive in competitive markets.

  • Workshops and bootcamps on startup fundamentals
  • Online courses and toolkits for continuous learning
  • Leadership development tracks for scaling teams
  • Practical case studies and founder stories

Additional Support Programs

Community Building

A Pan-African network of founders, investors, and mentors. We foster collaboration, shared learning, and partnerships across borders.

Events & Demo Days

Regular events, pitch competitions, and demo days where founders showcase their startups to investors, partners, and the wider ecosystem.
